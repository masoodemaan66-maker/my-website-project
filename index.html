This is why you should report a bear in need:

Bears are iconic animals, part of our natural world, but they are facing many challenges. Habitat destruction and loss of suitable space to live, human-caused mortality like illegal hunting, trade in bear parts for traditional medicines, but also keeping bears as pets or in private 'zoos'. Cubs are illegally traded and end up \behind bars, living their lives under unsuitable and in miserable conditions. By reporting any captive bear that you come across, you can help us to help them. Your report will support us to take the appropriate action and prevent other bears from ending up in similar conditions.

About

Bear Alert is developed by Bears in Mind & Foundation for Bears to keep track of the many captive bears, often living under horrible conditions, in order to help as many individuals as possible.

Over time, both organizations gathered information on more than 850 (with some overlap) of these bears in captivity. Some of them have already been rescued from their miserable existence and brought to a proper facility where the much needed care and space could be offered. Other bears unfortunately died before we could do anything. Some individuals vanished... But many are still waiting for a better life.
