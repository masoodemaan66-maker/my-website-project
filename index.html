Bear Alert

Have you seen a captive bear and are you concerned about its welfare? If so, we want to hear from you!

By reporting a captive bear that you believe is in trouble, you can help us to help the bear. Your report will support us to take the necessary action and hopefully it marks the start of a new life for this bear.

Thank you for caring and taking the time to help us help them.

This is why you should report a bear in need:

Bears are iconic animals, part of our natural world, but they are facing many challenges. Habitat destruction and loss of suitable space to live, human-caused mortality like illegal hunting, trade in bear parts for traditional medicines, but also keeping bears as pets or in private 'zoos'. Cubs are illegally traded and end up \behind bars, living their lives under unsuitable and in miserable conditions. By reporting any captive bear that you come across, you can help us to help them. Your report will support us to take the appropriate action and prevent other bears from ending up in similar conditions.

About

Bear Alert is developed by Bears in Mind & Foundation for Bears to keep track of the many captive bears, often living under horrible conditions, in order to help as many individuals as possible.

Over time, both organizations gathered information on more than 850 (with some overlap) of these bears in captivity. Some of them have already been rescued from their miserable existence and brought to a proper facility where the much-needed care and space could be offered. Others unfortunately died before we could do anything. Some individuals vanished... but many are still waiting for a better life.

Bear Alert is developed by bearsinmind.org & baer.de to keep track of the many captive bears.
